Telegram 18+ Channel Bot Policy

1. Purpose

This bot helps manage this 18+ Telegram channel. It moderates content, verifies age, enforces rules, and keeps the space respectful for adult users.

2. Age Verification

  • You must be 18+ to access or interact with this channel.
  • By joining, you confirm you're of legal age in your region.
  • The bot may ask for confirmation before giving access.

3. User Conduct

Prohibited behavior includes:

  • Sharing illegal or non-consensual content
  • Doxxing or sharing private info
  • Spamming or advertising without approval
  • Promoting hate speech, violence, or harm

4. Content Guidelines

  • Only legal, consensual adult content is allowed.
  • Strictly no content involving minors (real or fictional).
  • Follow Telegram's Terms of Service.

5. Bot Permissions

  • Warn or remove rule-breakers
  • Auto-delete spam or flagged content
  • Notify admins of suspicious actions

6. Reporting

Use the `/report` command to flag content or users to admins.

7. Disclaimer

The admins and bot aren't liable for user content, but action will be taken when violations are reported.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated without notice. Continued use means you accept the latest version.
